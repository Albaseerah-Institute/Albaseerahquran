Course Objective

Why Join Before Ramadan?

Ramadan is the month of the Qur'an. Begin now so that when the blessed month arrives, you can read with confidence, clarity, and devotion. Do not wait until Ramadan to start what you can begin today.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this course for? +
This course is designed for beginners and anyone who wants to learn or improve their Qur’an recitation.
Do I need any prior knowledge? +
No prior knowledge is required. We start from the basics and build up gradually.
How long is the course? +
The course runs for 12 weeks, with structured lessons and regular practice sessions.
Is the class live or recorded? +
The class is conducted live, and recordings may be provided where necessary.
How do I join the class since the main course is free? +
After completing the registration form, you will be added directly to the Whatsapp class group. The general course is completely **free**, and no payment is required to join.
Are there any paid options for more personalised learning? +
Yes. While the main course is free, students who want weekly one-on-one sessions with the tutor can subscribe to the paid individual class option for personalised correction and faster improvement.
Is attendance compulsory for the live lessons? +
Yes. Attendance to the live sessions is strictly compulsory to ensure proper follow-up, correction, and steady progress throughout the course.
Is there an age limit for joining the course? +
Yes. The minimum age requirement is 9 years old. This ensures students can follow instructions, participate, and benefit fully.
Can I join the course from outside Nigeria? +
Yes, absolutely! Students from any country can join the course since all sessions are online.
